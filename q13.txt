Q13 For all projects starting after Sep 1, 2021, find the project number and the IDs and names of all workers assigned to them.
SELECT PROJECT.PROJNO, WORKER.FIRSTNAME, WORKER.LASTNAME, WORKER.EMPID
FROM ASSIGN
INNER JOIN PROJECT
ON PROJECT.PROJNO = ASSIGN.PROJNO
INNER JOIN WORKER
ON ASSIGN.EMPID = WORKER.EMPID
WHERE PROJECT.STARTDATE > '01-09-2021'
ORDER BY PROJECT.PROJNO;
